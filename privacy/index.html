PaintPals Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

PaintPals and PaintPals Mini ("we", "our", or "the App") are children's coloring and drawing applications designed for kids ages 3-12. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable laws.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from children.

  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No photos (camera is used only for local on-device line art conversion)
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers for tracking purposes

Data Storage

All artwork, drawings, and app preferences are stored locally on the device only. We do not transmit any user-created content to external servers.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

  • No ads are displayed in the App
  • No behavioral data is collected or shared
  • Apple's StoreKit is used solely for processing in-app purchases

In-App Purchases

PaintPals offers optional subscription plans (Monthly and Yearly) managed entirely through Apple's App Store. Purchase transactions are handled by Apple and subject to Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to payment information.

Parental Controls

The App includes built-in parental controls:

  • Math-based parental gate for settings access
  • Configurable daily time limits
  • Purchase restriction toggle
  • External links require parental verification

Camera Usage

PaintPals uses the device camera solely for the "Photo to Line Art" feature, which converts photos into coloring templates. All processing happens on-device. Photos are not stored, uploaded, or shared.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our App is designed for and directed at children. We:

  • Do not collect personal information from children under 13
  • Do not allow children to make information publicly available
  • Do not use persistent identifiers for behavioral targeting
  • Do not include third-party advertising or analytics

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
